Aubergine Lasagna

Description

This delicious aubergine lasagna is the perfect comfort food dish – layers of roasted aubergines, creamy bechamel sauce and mozzarella cheese, all baked to golden perfection!

Ingredients

  • 2 large aubergines, cut into slices
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g minced beef
  • 400g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • For the bechamel sauce:
    • 50g butter
    • 50g plain flour
    • 500ml milk
    • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
    • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
    • 100g mozzarella cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F.
  2. Place the aubergine slices on a baking sheet and season with salt and pepper. Drizzle with the olive oil and bake in the oven for 20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the remaining olive oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  4. Add the minced beef and cook for 8-10 minutes until browned.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, oregano, tomato puree and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  6. To make the bechamel sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the flour and stir until combined. Gradually add the milk, stirring continuously until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Add the nutmeg and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the Parmesan cheese.
  8. To assemble, spread a layer of the beef mixture in the bottom of an ovenproof dish. Top with a layer of aubergines, followed by a layer of bechamel sauce. Repeat until all the ingredients have been used, finishing with a layer of bechamel sauce.
  9. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the top and bake in the oven for 30 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  10. Serve hot. Enjoy!
